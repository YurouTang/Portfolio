SonicPi


SonicPi is a tool for creating music from code. As a programmer who loves music, it was an interesting experience making my first composition with SonicPi. The experience opened up a different way to think about music composition for me, and also introduced me to novel ways to manipulate sound, such as the incorporation of randomness in music.







Link to official SonicPi page: SonicPi

OpenMusic


OpenMusic is a visual programming language for computer-assisted music composition. Meaning, you could use the tools included to generate sounds which could inspire your music composition. I had fun making avant-gard sounding music for the first time with OpenMusic.







Link to official OpenMusic page: OpenMusic
